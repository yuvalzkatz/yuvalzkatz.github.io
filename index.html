I'm a PhD candidate in the Linguistics Department at Tel Aviv University, supervised by Naama Friedmann.

My research explores the architecture of grammar through theoretical investigations of syntax and morphology and the study of aphasia.

I study aphasia and cognitive neuropsychology to gain a deeper understanding of the architecture of grammar, and use linguistic theory to better understand language impairments and refine their diagnostic and classification methods.
